HOW TO VISIT

From KL Sentral & Pudu Terminal

From KL Sentral:

  • • Take the Kelana Jaya LRT route to the Central Market LRT.
  • • Across the Gombak river to Dayabumi, Jalan Tun Sambanthan.
  • •National Textile Museum in front of Merdeka Square, Jalan Lengkok Raja.



















From Pudu terminal:


  • • Walk to Jalan Pudu LRT stop.
  • • Take RapidKL B112 bus heading to the Art Market.
  • • Walk to Tower Dayabumi, Jalan Tun Sambanthan.
  • • National Textile Museum in front of Merdeka Square, Jalan Lengkok Raja.
